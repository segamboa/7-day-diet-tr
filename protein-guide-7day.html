Foundation principles · all regions
Eat protein first
JJ Virgin: lead every meal with your protein. It controls hunger hormones, stabilises blood sugar, and guarantees you hit 30g minimum before anything else.
Identity-based rituals
Tony Robbins: link your food choices to who you are — "I'm someone who performs at the highest level." 60 seconds of incantation before meals transforms compliance into identity.
Batch cook on Sundays
Two hours on Sunday eliminates daily decision-making. When healthy food is the easiest choice, you'll make it every time. Prep proteins, grains, and snack bags in one session.
No sensitive foods
JJ Virgin: remove top food sensitivities for 3 weeks first. Items marked * (eggs, dairy) in the cheat sheet require completing the Virgin Diet before reintroducing.
Progress, not perfection
Robbins: raise your standard, not your stress. Miss a protein goal? Adjust the next meal — never skip two meals in a row. One bad meal is an event, not a pattern.
Measure what matters
Track protein grams, not calories. When you consistently hit protein targets, macros naturally fall into place. Use the cheat sheet's oz targets as your field guide.
